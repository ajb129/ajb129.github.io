Exploring Layers and Ripples

Studying English with Computer Parsing



Alastair Butler

Hirosaki University






This book provides Definite Clause Grammar (DCG) definitions that are executable code for the XSB Tabling Prolog system. The definitions create a wide coverage parsing system that gives structural feedback automatically while doubling as a descriptive grammar of contemporary English. The adopted modelling supposes that words appear under layers of tree structure assembling connections to all words. The parsing activity discovers these layers while obeying ripple effects that constrain surrounding words to form grammatical utterances.
