Exploring Layers and Ripples

A Parsing Grammar of English



Alastair Butler

Hirosaki University






This book provides Definite Clause Grammar (DCG) definitions that are executable code for the XSB Tabling Prolog system. The definitions create a wide coverage parsing system that gives structural feedback automatically while doubling as a descriptive grammar of contemporary English. The starting assumption is that words have ripple effects that constrain surrounding words to form utterances. The adopted modelling supposes that words appear under layers building up a connected tree structure for all utterance content, with parsing as the activity of identifying layers while obeying ripple effect consequences.
