Unit 23
Relative clauses with Where and When; Reduced relative clauses

The adverbs where and when can be used as relative adverbs in relative clauses, annotated with the RADV tag. Other relative adverbs are whereby and how. Pattern (23.1) will find all the relative adverbs of the TSVT.

(23.1)
/^RADV/

23.1   Relative clauses with Where

  • CLAIM: Use where in relative clauses to modify a noun referring to a place. Look at the examples found with pattern (23.2) to check this claim.
(23.2)
/^RADV/ < /^where/

For example, (23.3) is found with (23.2).

(23.3)
we could do somethin fine out there , maybe find a place where no_one 's ever been .

23.2   Relative clauses with When

  • CLAIM: Use when in relative clauses to modify a noun referring to a time. Look at the examples found with pattern (23.4) to check this claim.
(23.4)
/^RADV/ < /^when/

For example, (23.5) is found with (23.4).

(23.5)
Last year , when he worked out at halfback all season , he did n't get into a single game .

The relative adverb when can be omitted when the relative clause is restrictive. Find such examples with pattern (23.6).

(23.6)
/^NP-TMP/ < /^\*T\*/

For example, (23.7) is found with (23.6).

(23.7)
you were n't like that last time I saw you

23.3   Reduced relative clauses with participle verb forms

Reduced relative clauses with participle verb forms are found with pattern (23.8).

(23.8)
/^IP-PPL/ > /^(NP|NLYR)\b/

This includes present participle verbs (VAG) to give a progressive construction, and past participle verbs (VVN) to give a passive construction.

  • Limiting (23.8) with the pattern of (23.9) gives examples of reduced relative clauses with VAG in a progressive construction.
(23.9)
/^IP-PPL/ > /^(NP|NLYR)\b/ < /^VAG/

For example, (23.10) is found with (23.9).

(23.10)
he looked like a cow chewing a piece of grass
  • Limiting (23.8) with the pattern of (23.11) gives examples of reduced relative clauses with VVN in a passive construction.
(23.11)
/^IP-PPL/ > /^(NP|NLYR)\b/ < /^VVN/ < /LGS/

For example, (23.12) is found with (23.11).

(23.12)
The site where this was is a little seaside town called Selsy Hill .
