Unit 23

Relative clauses with Where and When; Reduced relative clauses

The adverbs where and when can be used as relative adverbs in relative clauses, annotated with the RADV tag. Other relative adverbs are whereby and how. Pattern (23.1) will find all the relative adverbs of the TSVT.

(23.1)
/^RADV/

23.3    Reduced relative clauses with participle verb forms

Reduced relative clauses with participle verb forms are found with pattern (23.8).

(23.8)
/^IP-PPL/ > /^(NP|NLYR)\b/

This includes present participle verbs (VAG) to give a progressive construction, and past participle verbs (VVN) to give a passive construction.
