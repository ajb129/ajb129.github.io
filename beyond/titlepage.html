Help with finding constructions of Grammar and Beyond 3B in the TSPC

Alastair Butler

Hirosaki University





Grammar and Beyond 3B by Laurie Blass, Susan Iannuzzi, Alice Savage, and Randi Reppen is a textbook published by Cambridge University Press. The Treebank Semantics Parsed Corpus (TSPC) is a corpus of English with hand-worked analysis for half-a-million words. The purpose of these notes is to connect these two resources with TGrep2 search patterns.
