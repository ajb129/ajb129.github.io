Help with finding constructions of Grammar and Beyond 3B in the TSPC



Alastair Butler

Hirosaki University






Grammar and Beyond 3B by Laurie Blass, Susan Iannuzzi, Alice Savage, and Randi Reppen is a textbook published by Cambridge University Press. The Treebank Semantics Parsed Corpus (TSPC) is a corpus of English with hand-worked analysis for half-a-million words. These notes use TGrep2 search patterns to connect these two resources.
