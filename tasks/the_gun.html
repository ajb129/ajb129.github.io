Week 2 Task

Read:

  • The Gun by Philip K. Dick

In your groups, prepare answers to the following questions.

1.
Why do the "trespassers" travel to the planet? What did they hope to find?
2.
Why does the search party leave the spaceship? How well prepared are they for what they might find? Do they have any armaments?
3.
What does the search party conclude from the city, gun, and vault that they find? Do you think their understanding is likely to be correct?
4.
Why do the "trespassers" hope to return to the planet? What can we expect to happen if they return?

Week 2 Homework

Write answers to TWO of the above four questions.

Answers need to be self-contained. You should structure your answers as a single essay that:

  • provides a clear introduction of what you are attempting
  • follows the introduction with the discussion of your answers
  • ends with an overall conclusion that summarises what you have achieved
