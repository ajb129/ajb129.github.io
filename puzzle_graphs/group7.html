Group 7, Puzzle 1Proceed along the main Swafford to Hardley road and turn left to Burfell just before you reach Hardley.
Group 7, Puzzle 2Continue along this road until you arrive at a car park.
Group 7, Puzzle 3Go through a turnstile at the back of the car park and follow the footpath past Garston Hall.
Group 7, Puzzle 4Continue until you cross over the Burfell road and then take the track that leads up into the wood.
Group 7, Puzzle 5Once you are inside the wood keep the fence on your left.
Group 7, Puzzle 6If you're lucky, you may see some horses behind the fence.
Group 7, Puzzle 7Coming out of the wood take the footpath which passes by an old ruined house.
Group 7, Puzzle 8Continue for about a mile until you reach a picnic site.
Group 7, Puzzle 9From here you have uninterrupted views over the lake.
Group 7, Puzzle 10Leaving the picnic site take the path which climbs up, keeping Brooks Farm on your left.
Group 7, Puzzle 11Follow the ridge for about two miles.
Group 7, Puzzle 12You will be able to see Garside Fell in the far distance.
Group 7, Puzzle 13The car park is now a few hundred yards away.
