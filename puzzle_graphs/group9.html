Group 9, Puzzle 2Everyone picked them up, and put them in a bag that was lying around.
Group 9, Puzzle 3That big bag was full of them, but he somehow crammed it into his rucksack.

Group 9, Puzzle 5“Teacher! Are you OK?”; we asked him in surprised voices.
Group 9, Puzzle 6Taking care so as not to fall down, he started walking over the sand.
