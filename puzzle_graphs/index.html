English Puzzle Graphs

To find out about English Puzzle Graphs, please read the introduction.

To attempt a puzzle, click on one of the puzzle links below. Then, try turning the graph of words into ‘solutions’ that are either a sentence fragment, a single sentence or multiple sentences of English.

To check your success, click the ‘Model answer’ toggle link for a puzzle.

From a puzzle, you can navigate to the next or previous puzzle of the same puzzle group. Each puzzle also has a link for returning to this page, which lists all available puzzles.
